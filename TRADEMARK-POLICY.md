1. PERMITTED USE

You may use the StreamKernel name without permission for:

  · Referring to StreamKernel in documentation, blog posts,
    articles, presentations, or educational materials

  · Stating factual compatibility, such as:
      "Compatible with StreamKernel"
      "Built on StreamKernel"
      "Built on StreamKernel SDK"
      "Built using StreamKernel SPI"
      "StreamKernel plugin for [Product]"
      "StreamKernel connector for [Product]"
      "StreamKernel integration for [Product]"
      "Powered by StreamKernel"

  · Academic, research, and non-commercial educational use

  · Describing integrations, connectors, plugins, or
    extensions for StreamKernel

  · Comparative statements, such as:
      "Alternative to StreamKernel"
      "Compared to StreamKernel"

Such use must:
  · Be truthful and non-misleading
  · Not imply endorsement or official affiliation
  · Not create confusion about ownership

2. PROHIBITED USE

Without explicit written permission, you may not:

  · Use "StreamKernel" in the name of a competing product,
    framework, or platform

  · Use "StreamKernel" in the name of a fork, modified
    distribution, or derivative runtime

  · Use "StreamKernel" in a company name, product name,
    or service name

  · Use "StreamKernel" in domain names, including:
      streamkernel-something.com
      mystreamkernel.io
      streamkernelcloud.com
    Note: Domain names for community plugins or connectors
    may be permitted with written approval. Contact
    licensing@streamkernel.io.

  · Use "StreamKernel" in a way that implies:
      Official endorsement
      Partnership
      Certification
      Official distribution

  · Use the StreamKernel logo in commercial contexts
    without written permission

  · Create branding or visual identity that is confusingly
    similar to the StreamKernel Mark

The designation "Certified for StreamKernel" and
"StreamKernel Certified [Plugin Name]" are reserved
exclusively for participants in the official StreamKernel
Partner and Certification Program.

This program does not yet exist. When launched, it will
be announced at https://streamkernel.io. Until then, no
party may use "Certified for StreamKernel" or any
confusingly similar designation.

If you create a fork or modified version of StreamKernel:

  · You must not use the "StreamKernel" name in the
    project title

  · You must clearly identify the fork as unofficial

Example:

  Not Acceptable: "FastStreamKernel"
